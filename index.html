About us!

Welcome to The Woodland Wag – where tails wag and paws feel at home!

Nestled in the heart of beautiful Cannock Chase, The Woodland Wag is a trusted dog sitting service with over 6 years of hands-on experience caring for dogs of all breeds, sizes, and personalities. Whether your pup is full of energy or prefers a peaceful cuddle, we provide a safe, loving, and comfortable environment tailored to their unique needs.
Our passion for dogs is at the core of everything we do. From playful walks through the woodland trails to cozy evenings and attentive care, we treat every dog as if they were our own. We understand how important it is to have peace of mind when you're away, which is why we focus on trust, safety, and genuine affection in every stay.
Whether it’s for a weekend, a holiday, or just the day, you can count on The Woodland Wag to keep your furry friend happy, healthy, and tail-wagging!
